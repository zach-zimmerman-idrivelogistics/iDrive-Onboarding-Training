Module 2

Logistics Flow + iDrive

Walk through the complete client onboarding flow — from the moment a deal is won to final rate verification. Click any step to expand it, or use the buttons to step through the entire flow in order.

Click any step — or use the buttons to walk through the full flow
🎯 Phase 1 — Deal Trigger
1
01🤝Deal Won by Sales▾
The revenue team closes the deal in HubSpot. This is the single trigger for the entire onboarding process — every downstream step depends on this event firing correctly.
2
02📢Auto Slack Posted in #closed-won Channel▾
HubSpot automatically posts a deal summary to the #closed-won Slack channel. Save this post link — you'll reference the carrier list from this exact post in Step 11 when requesting carrier accounts.
3
03📄Completed DocuSign Arrives in Onboarding Inbox▾
The completed DocuSign agreement lands in onboarding@idrivelogistics.com. This is your primary source document for the entire onboarding — all system setups, channel screenshots, and account details flow from here.
4
04🔍DocuSign Contains Payment Info + Shipping Location Info▾
Open and review the DocuSign. Locate three key sections: (1) billing/payment information, (2) the 3PL's shipping facility address and location details, and (3) TMS setup contact information. All three are referenced in later steps.
💬 Phase 2 — SETUP Slack Channel
5
05💬Create the SETUP Slack Channel▾
Create a dedicated Slack channel using the naming format: #setup-[3pl-name] (lowercase, hyphens, no spaces). Set the channel description to include the 3PL's name, WMS type, and go-live target date.
6
06👥Add Product, Sales, CX & Jen to the Channel▾
Add all four required standing members to every SETUP channel: Product, Sales, CX, and Jen. Tag each member with a brief intro so they understand why they've been added and what role they'll play in this onboarding.
7
07📸Screenshot Shipping Location Info → SETUP Channel▾
From the DocuSign in the Onboarding Inbox, screenshot the Shipping Location Info section and post it into the new SETUP channel. Label it clearly: "📍 Shipping Location — [3PL Name] — [Date]".
8
08📸Screenshot TMS Setup Contacts → SETUP Channel▾
Screenshot the TMS Setup Contact Info from the DocuSign and post it in the SETUP channel. Label it: "👤 TMS Setup Contact — [3PL Name] — [Date]". This is the contact the TMS integration team will reach for setup coordination.
✉️ Phase 3 — Email & Account Requests
9
09✉️Request Alias Email from Kaylynn (if not pre-set up)▾
Message Kaylynn to request the alias email for this 3PL account, if one hasn't already been created. Confirm whether it's a new request or pre-existing. The alias email becomes the EZYP account login credential.
10
10📧Request Claims Email from Kaylynn (if not pre-set up)▾
Also request the claims email address from Kaylynn — this is a separate email from the alias email. It will be provided to Sharlynn's team for claims processing. Request both emails at the same time to save a round-trip.
⏰
⏰
Wait 24 Hours — Emails Must Be Active Before Proceeding
Both the Alias Email and Claims Email must fully propagate before you can create the EZYP account. If Kaylynn confirms the emails were pre-created, proceed immediately. If newly requested, wait the full 24 hours — attempting EZYP setup too early will fail. Use this window to complete Step 11.
11
11🚚Request Carrier Accounts Listed in #closed-won Channel▾
Return to the #closed-won Slack post from Step 2. Identify all carrier accounts listed for this 3PL and submit requests for each one. This step can be completed during the 24-hour email wait window.
12
12⚡Setup EZYP Account with the Alias Email▾
Once the alias email is confirmed active, create the EZYP account using that alias email as the login credential. Complete all required fields from the 3PL's DocuSign data. Record the account credentials — they'll be referenced in the SMC/IIC setup.
13
13📋Update SETUP Slack Channel with Carrier Account Requests▾
Post in the SETUP channel with the full carrier account list. Use clear status labels: ✅ Active (account exists, credentials available) vs. ⏳ Requested (submitted, pending setup). Update this post every day as statuses change.
14
14📤Provide Claims Email to Sharlynn▾
Send Sharlynn the claims email address directly. Her team handles claims processing and needs this address to configure their workflow for this account. Do this as soon as the email is confirmed active — don't wait until the end of onboarding.
🖥 Phase 4 — System Setup
15
15🖥Setup SMC / IIC▾
Create the 3PL's record in SMC/IIC using their DocuSign information. Verify business name, primary contact, account type, and carrier associations before saving. The SMC record details are required for the billing forwarding email in Step 20 — record the account name and ID now.
16
16💼Setup NetSuite▾
Set up the 3PL's billing account in NetSuite using the payment and billing details from the DocuSign. Confirm billing contact name, address, and payment terms match the DocuSign exactly. Flag any discrepancies before finalizing.
17
17🚛Setup TMS 3PL Tenant▾
Provision the new 3PL tenant in the TMS platform following the WMS-specific setup guide. Configure all required tenant settings. Record the Tenant ID and add it to the 3PL's tab in the WMS Google Sheet (Step 19).
18
18📍Setup Shipping Site Location from Onboarding Form▾
Set up the 3PL's shipping facility location using the address from the Onboarding Form (DocuSign). This location is critical — the TMS uses it for carrier account assignment and rate lookup. Verify the address is exact before saving.
19
19📊Add 3PL & Brand Info to WMS Google Sheet▾
Add a new tab in the WMS-specific Google Sheet for internal tracking. Include: 3PL name, associated brand(s), contacts, TMS Tenant ID, EZYP account info, and onboarding date. This sheet is the team's internal reference for this account going forward.
💸 Phase 5 — Billing & Admin
20
20💸Forward DocuSign to Billing Inbox + Include SMC Details▾
Forward the completed DocuSign to the Billing inbox. Critical: The body of the forwarding email must include the key details from the SMC record created in Step 15 — account name, account ID, and any other relevant billing reference information. Do not forward without this content in the email body.
✅ Phase 6 — Verification
21
21✅Verify Carriers in SMC Are Imported to TMS▾
Confirm that all carrier accounts set up in SMC have been successfully imported and synced to the TMS. Every carrier must appear and be active in both systems. If any are missing, resolve before moving to location setup.
22
22🗺️Set Location for Accounts for Each Brand in TMS▾
In the TMS, assign the correct shipping site location (from Step 18) to each brand's account associated with this 3PL. Each brand requires its own individual location assignment — do not assume one assignment covers all brands.
23
23🔗Verify / Complete Integration Steps (Unique to Each WMS)▾
Complete the integration verification steps specific to this 3PL's WMS platform. These steps are unique to each WMS — there is no universal checklist. Refer to the WMS-specific integration guide for this platform and confirm all steps are complete before rate verification.
24
24🎯Post in Slack — Have 3PL Verify They Can See Rates▾
Post in the SETUP Slack channel asking Sales to have the 3PL log into the system and verify two things: (1) they can see their carrier rates in the TMS, and (2) the rates match the rate charts provided by the carriers. This confirmation closes the onboarding loop.
🔔
Ongoing — Keep the SETUP Slack Channel Updated
Every time a new carrier has been requested or added, post an update in the SETUP channel with the current status. If you need information at any point during onboarding, ask directly in the relevant channels — don't wait. The team can't help if they don't know you're blocked.
