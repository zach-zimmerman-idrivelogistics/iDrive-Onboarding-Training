Module 1 — Locate the Alias Email List
Claims Email Setup

Locate the Alias Email List

Before creating a claims email, you need to access the shared alias email spreadsheet in Google Drive. This sheet tracks all assigned emails and is your reference before adding a new one.

💬
Step 1 — Open Slack
  • 1
    Open Slack
    Launch Slack if it isn't already open. You'll be starting from the VAP Onboarding Team chat.
  • 2
    Open the VAP Onboarding Team Chat
    Navigate to the VAP Onboarding Team group message or channel in your Slack sidebar.
  • 3
    Click the Google Drive Links at the Top
    At the top of the VAP Onboarding Team chat, you'll see pinned or posted Google Drive links. Click on those links to open the shared folder.
  • 4
    Open the Alias Email List
    Find and open the Alias Email List from the Google Drive folder. This takes you to the spreadsheet where all alias and claims emails are tracked.
✅ Good to Know

You should now see a Google Sheets page with access to the shared email tracking spreadsheet. If you receive a permissions error, contact Kaylynn Caldwell to have access granted before proceeding.

Claims Email Setup

Identify the Client's WMS

The claims email name includes the WMS type. Before creating the email, you need to verify which WMS the client uses by checking the #closed-won-customers Slack channel.

📋
Step 2 — Check the Alias Tab & Find the WMS
  • 1
    Navigate to the "Alias for Claims Inbox" Tab
    At the bottom of the Google Sheets spreadsheet, locate and click the tab labeled Alias for Claims Inbox. Make sure you are on this specific tab before proceeding.
  • 2
    Go to the #closed-won-customers Slack Channel
    Open Slack and navigate to the #closed-won-customers channel. Find the post for the customer you are currently setting up a claims email for.
  • 3
    Note the WMS Listed for This Customer
    In the closed-won post, locate the WMS (Warehouse Management System) listed for this customer. You will need this exact WMS name when creating the claims email address in the next module.
⚠️ Important

The WMS name used in the email address must match exactly what is listed in the #closed-won post. Using the wrong WMS name or abbreviation will cause a mismatch in the tracking sheet. When in doubt, refer directly to the closed-won post rather than guessing.

Find the Shipping State

The claims email address also includes the state abbreviation of the client's shipping facility. You'll locate this from the onboarding PDF inside Outlook.

✉️
Step 3 — Check Outlook for the Onboarding Email
  • 1
    Open Outlook
    Open Microsoft Outlook and navigate to the Onboarding email inbox. This is the shared inbox where completed DocuSign agreements arrive.
  • 2
    Find the Closed Email for This Customer
    Search for or scroll to find the same customer's closed/completed email that you identified in the previous step from #closed-won-customers.
  • 3
    Open the Info PDF Attachment
    Inside that email, locate and open the Info PDF attachment. This document contains all the key setup details for this client, including their shipping address.
  • 4
    Note the State Under Shipping Info
    Within the PDF, find the Shipping Info section. Note the state abbreviation (e.g. MI, TX, CA) listed for the company's shipping location. You will use this in the email address.

📌 Write down both the WMS name (from Module 2) and the state abbreviation (from this step) before moving on. You'll need both to create the email in the next module.

Claims Email Setup

Create the Claims Email Address

Now that you have the WMS name and the state abbreviation, you're ready to create the claims email address and capture a screenshot to send to Kaylynn.

📧
Step 4 — Build & Create the Email Address

Use the following format to construct the claims email address:

📋 Email Format
WMS-companyname-state-claims@idrivelogistics.com
Example: Packiyo-Encore-MI-claims@idrivelogistics.com
  • 1
    Create the Email Address
    Using the WMS, company name, and state you've collected, construct the claims email address following the format above. Enter it in the appropriate field as shown in the reference screenshot.
  • 2
    Take a Screenshot with Windows + Shift + S
    Press Windows + Shift + S to open the Snipping Tool overlay. You'll take a screenshot of what you've just created to share with Kaylynn in the next step.
  • 3
    Open the Snipping Tool Notification
    After pressing the shortcut, a notification will appear in your notification center (click the timestamp in the bottom-right corner of your screen). Click the Snipping Tool notification to open the tool.
  • 4
    Click the Square and Circle Box Icon
    In Snipping Tool, click the square and circle box icon (the freeform/annotate button). This enables you to draw and annotate on your screenshot before copying it.
  • 5
    Ensure Settings Match the Reference & Draw Two Boxes
    Verify your Snipping Tool settings match what's shown in the reference screenshot. Then click and drag to draw two annotation boxes highlighting the relevant fields, as shown.
  • 6
    Copy with Ctrl + C
    Once your annotation boxes are drawn, press Ctrl + C to copy the annotated screenshot to your clipboard. You'll paste this into your Slack message to Kaylynn in the next module.

Claims Email Setup

Message Kaylynn in Slack

With your annotated screenshot copied to clipboard, you'll now send Kaylynn Caldwell a Slack message requesting that she create the claims email — including the sheet link and screenshot.

💬
Step 5 — Send the Request to Kaylynn
  • 1
    Search for Kaylynn Caldwell in Slack
    In the Slack search bar at the top of the window, type Kaylynn Caldwell and search for her name.
  • 2
    Click Her Name to Start a Direct Message
    From the search results, click her name to open a direct message conversation with Kaylynn.
  • 3
    Paste the Screenshot with Ctrl + V
    Click in the message text box and press Ctrl + V to paste the annotated screenshot you copied in the previous module.
  • 4
    Go Back and Copy the Google Sheet Link
    Return to the Google Sheet you were working in (the Alias Email List). Copy the URL from the browser address bar.
  • 5
    Paste the Sheet Link into the Message
    Back in the Slack DM with Kaylynn, paste the Google Sheet link into the text box alongside the screenshot.
  • 6
    Press Ctrl + Enter for a New Line
    Hold Ctrl + Enter to start a new line in the message without sending it. This allows you to add your written message below the link and screenshot.
  • 7
    Type Your Request Message & Send
    Type a short, polite message — or use the example below. Ensure the screenshot and sheet link are both in the message, then click Send.
💬 Suggested Message (feel free to use this)
Good afternoon Kaylynn! Can you create this claims email when you have time? Thanks!
📎 Before sending, verify: The Google Sheet link is pasted in the message AND the annotated screenshot is attached. Both are required for Kaylynn to process the request.

Claims Email Setup

Update the WMS Google Sheet

The final step is to log the new claims email in the WMS Onboarding tracking sheet, then proceed to Easypost setup for this specific email address.

📊
Step 6 — Log the Email in the Tracking Sheet
  • 1
    Return to the Google Sheet
    Go back to the Google Sheets spreadsheet you were working in earlier (the one with the Alias Email List).
  • 2
    Switch to the WMSOnboarding Tab
    At the bottom of the spreadsheet, click the WMSOnboarding tab. Make sure you are on this specific tab, not the Alias for Claims Inbox tab from earlier.
  • 3
    Add the Business Name to the Next Open Spot
    Find the next open/empty row in the list and add the business name for the company you're onboarding. This reserves their slot and keeps the sheet in order.
📌 Note for Training

Because the example company uses Packiyo, the training exercise uses the next available email slot. For your actual onboarding, use whatever company you are currently setting up — do not use the training example company in the live sheet.

⚡
Next Step — Proceed to Easypost Setup

Now that the claims email has been designated and logged, follow the Easypost Training instructions to get this specific email set up in Easypost.

📬 Because the claims email is formatted as WMSOnboarding@idrivelogistics.com, the Easypost verification code will be sent to the WMSOnboarding email in your Outlook. Ensure Kaylynn Caldwell has given you access to whatever onboarding emails you need before starting Easypost setup. Follow the Easypost instructions exactly.

✅ You're All Set

Once you've completed the Easypost setup for this claims email, the claims email onboarding for this client is complete. Good luck!
